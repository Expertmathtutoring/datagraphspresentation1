Integrated Data Graphs and Plots

Bar Graph (Horizontal, Discrete Data)
Compare discrete categories. Bars separated.
Column Graph (Vertical)
Vertical bars, like bar graph, for discrete categories.
Line Graph
Shows trends over time.
Pie Chart
Portions/percent of a whole.
Histogram (No Gaps)
Distribution of continuous numerical data (bins adjacent).
Scatter Plot
Relationship between two numeric variables.
Area Graph
Quantities over time, area filled under line.
Bubble Chart
Visualises three variables: x, y, size.
Dot Plot
Uses dots for frequencies/values.
Box and Whisker Plot (Box Plot)
Spread, quartiles, median, outliers.
Stem-and-Leaf Plot

    
Distribution, keeps actual values.

  


  

    
Frequency Polygon

    
    
Line connecting midpoints of histogram bins.

  


  

    
Time Series Graph

    
    
Data over intervals/time.

  


  

    
Venn Diagram

    
    
Relationships/intersection between sets.

  


  

    
Pareto Chart

    
    
Bar chart sorted, cumulative % shows overall effect.
